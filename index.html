About Us

Furniture Co. is a family-owned business that has been providing quality furniture to our customers for over 50 years. Our mission is to create beautiful and functional pieces that will last for generations.

Our History

Our story began in 1970, when our founder, John Smith, opened a small furniture store in downtown Seattle. With his passion for design and craftsmanship, he quickly gained a reputation for creating unique and durable pieces that were both stylish and functional. Over the years, our business grew, and we expanded our product line to include a wide range of furniture styles.

Our Team

At Furniture Co., we are proud to have a team of skilled craftsmen and designers who are passionate about their work. From the initial design concept to the final product, we work together to create pieces that are both beautiful and functional.

Our Philosophy

At the heart of our philosophy is a commitment to quality and craftsmanship. We believe that furniture should not only look great, but also stand the test of time. That's why we use only the finest materials and techniques in our manufacturing process.
